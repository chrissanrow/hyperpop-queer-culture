Hyperpop and queer culture of the 2020s

Hyperpop, an almagamation of various experimental (largely internet-based) microgenres with heavy electronic and experimental influences rose to popularity around the early 2020s. It is characterized, among other things, by heavy production and intense vocal processing.

This educational resource explores the connection of this emergent genre with queer culture and expression of queer identities. The resource will somewhat emphasize the digicore subgenre of hyperpop, whose queer reading is less widely represented in literature.
